Administrative levels

These checkboxes filter which CHGIS layers are searched and mapped.

  • Text-passage search finds possible CHGIS names and lets you uncheck candidates beside the map.
  • Place-name search maps the names you enter directly; separate multiple names with commas.
  • Dates and administrative levels filter both kinds of search.
  • Nearby or identical locations are grouped into one marker with all records in the popup.
