How It Works

The app scans pasted text against CHGIS names, including simplified/traditional variants and short administrative aliases. Candidate names are shown beside the map and can be unchecked.

Date filters are applied before candidates are mapped. If a detected name has no active CHGIS record in the selected year or range, it is excluded from the candidate list.

Notes

  • Use traditional Chinese for manual place-name searches when possible.
  • Separate manual names with commas, either "," or "，".
  • Nearby or identical locations are grouped into one marker with all CHGIS records in the popup.
  • CHGIS links in popups open the corresponding placename search.
